Conferences — where researchers surface

NeurIPS

Annual · Dec · Vancouver / virtualFamily A

Largest ML conference. Target poster presenters, workshop speakers, and oral paper authors. Sponsor tracks give access to attendee lists. Best sourcing window: Oct–Nov pre-conference CFP period.

ICML

Annual · Jul · Rotating globalFamily A

Core ML theory and methods. Target authors in LLM, RL, and optimisation tracks. Workshop co-organisers are senior researchers open to relationships outside their current org.

ICLR

Annual · May · Rotating globalFamily A

Highly selective. Acceptance = strong signal. Outstanding paper award authors are top-1% targets. Spotlight and oral presentations are the primary sourcing list.

CVPR

Annual · Jun · Rotating USFamily A

Top computer vision venue. Critical for vision-language model researchers, video generation, and multimodal AI. 10,000+ attendees — focus on oral and best paper tracks.

ACL / EMNLP / NAACL

Annual · RotatingFamily A

NLP-specific conferences. Essential for LLM researchers, alignment work, and language understanding. EMNLP tends to attract more applied / industry-adjacent talent than ACL.

AAAI

Annual · Feb · Rotating USFamily A

Broad AI — planning, reasoning, knowledge representation. Useful for researchers working on agents, symbolic AI, and neuro-symbolic approaches alongside deep learning.

ECCV / ICCV

Biennial · Rotating Europe / globalFamily A

European / International computer vision. Strong for 3D, generative vision, and scene understanding researchers. Alternates with CVPR in even years.

CoRL / RSS

AnnualFamily A

Robotics + ML. Target if hiring for embodied AI, RL for robotics, or physical simulation research. Small, tight-knit community — relationships matter more than cold outreach here.

University labs — by research strength

Deep learning, NLP, robotics, computer vision. Strong PhD placement into industry.

Stanford HAI / SAIL

Foundation models, human-AI interaction, RL. Very strong industry connections via faculty.

CMU ML Dept / LTI

NLP, speech, computer vision, ML systems. Largest dedicated ML dept in US.

Berkeley BAIR / CHAI

RL, robotics, alignment. Home of PPO, RLHF foundations. Strong startup spinout culture.

Toronto / Vector Institute

Deep learning birthplace (Hinton). Vector Institute is a dedicated AI research hub — strong pipeline.

Mila — Quebec AI Institute

Yoshua Bengio's lab. Strong in generative models, GNNs, RL. Bilingual talent pool.

ETH Zürich AI Center

Computer vision, 3D AI, robotics. Top European pipeline. English-language research environment.

Oxford / Cambridge

NLP, Bayesian methods, AI safety. Strong for alignment-adjacent research talent.

NYU CDS / CILVR

Yann LeCun's group. Self-supervised learning, vision, energy-based models.

Conferences & communities — where engineers surface

AI Engineer Summit

Annual · San FranciscoFamily B

The primary conference for applied LLM / GenAI engineers. Speaker list is a sourcing goldmine — these are practitioners shipping production AI, not theorists. Highest-density event for this family.

Hacker News — Show HN & Who's Hiring

Monthly threadsFamily B

Show HN posts by engineers building LLM tools are pre-qualified outreach targets. Monthly "Who's hiring" threads surface active candidates. Search HN user history for AI-related comments.

LLM Meetups (local)

Ongoing · All major tech hubsFamily B

SF AI Tinkerers, NYC LLM meetup, London LLM meetup, Berlin AI builders. Speakers and regular attendees self-select as active practitioners. Sponsor or co-host to get attendee access.

Hugging Face community

Online + eventsFamily B

Model contributors, Space builders, dataset authors. Sort by downloads and activity. HF Discord and forums surface active contributors. HF community sprints are sourcing events in disguise.

Applied ML / MLOps Summit

AnnualFamily B

Practitioner-focused. Talks on RAG, evaluation frameworks, prompt engineering at scale, production LLM architecture. Less academic than NeurIPS — more directly relevant for Family B profiles.

Kaggle competitions

OngoingFamily B

NLP and LLM competition leaderboards surface strong applied ML engineers. Top-100 finishers on relevant competitions are worth direct outreach — they've demonstrated skill publicly.

Product Hunt / IndieHackers

OnlineFamily B

Engineers who've launched AI products independently demonstrate initiative, product sense, and full-stack LLM skill. Product Hunt "AI" category launches are a sourcing feed.

Y Combinator AI batch alumni

Biannual batchesFamily B

YC alumni from AI-focused startups that didn't scale or pivoted are high-conversion targets — strong skills, product experience, motivated to join a larger platform mission.

Conferences & communities — where infra engineers surface

MLSys

Annual · Mar · Rotating USFamily C

The definitive ML systems conference. Papers on model serving, training efficiency, distributed systems for ML, hardware-software co-design. Every speaker and author is a pre-qualified Tier 1 target.

NVIDIA GTC

Annual · Mar · San Jose + virtualFamily C

10,000+ GPU and AI infra engineers. Deep learning infrastructure sessions, CUDA developer talks, and DGX deployment tracks. Sponsor sessions give access to attendee and speaker lists.

KDD

Annual · Aug · Rotating globalFamily C

Data mining and ML at scale. Applied data science track surfaces senior ML platform engineers. Industry day speakers are practitioners, not academics — highest-value sourcing window at this event.

SC (Supercomputing)

Annual · Nov · Rotating USFamily C

The HPC community's main event. Surfaces GPU optimisation, distributed compute, and interconnect experts. Best pool for Tier 3 HPC-to-ML adjacency conversions. Technical program committee is a sourcing list.

SREcon

3x annual · Americas / EMEA / APACFamily C

SRE and infrastructure reliability community. Speakers running ML workload reliability, GPU observability, and model serving SLAs are prime Tier 4 adjacency candidates for Family C roles.

Ray Summit

Annual · San FranciscoFamily C

Distributed ML compute focused on Ray ecosystem. Speaker and contributor community maps directly onto ML infra engineering talent. GitHub contributor list for Ray itself is a parallel sourcing channel.

vLLM / Triton community

Online + summitsFamily C

Active GitHub contributors to vLLM, Triton, CUTLASS, FlashAttention, and DeepSpeed are Tier 1 inference and kernel engineering targets. Contributor lists are fully public and searchable.

PyData / Data Council

Annual · Multiple locationsFamily C

Surfaces data engineers building ML pipelines — the primary Tier 4 adjacent pool for feature store, data infra, and ML data platform roles. Speaker tracks on Spark, Kafka, and Arrow are the target sessions.
